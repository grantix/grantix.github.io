Chewy.com

Chewy.com is one of the world's leading pet supply delivery companies. Acquired in 2017 by PetSmart for $3.35 billion, it was valuable long before the Pandemic. Since March, however, Chewy's valuation has soared over $20 billion!

Hover your mouse over the graph to see more details!

It is no coincidence that Chewy began gaining value in the pandemic. No one spends more on their pets than new pet owners! Buying doggie beds, litter boxes, food and toys from an online purveyor boosts their company earnings enough to nearly double their valuation.

Since the rise of COVID-19 and the mass movement towards working from home, searches for Animal Shelters and specific pets have risen along with the market economy for delivered pet supplies. My hypothesis of increased interest in pet adoptions and companionship during this year's pandemic is correct!

2020 may have been a year of trials and tribulations for many of us, but in the eyes of the recently adopted Pandemic Pets, it was the best year ever.
