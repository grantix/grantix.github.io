Looking to adopt?

The first thing most people do when considering a pet is turn to the nearest search engine...Thankfully Google publishes trends for all search terms and enables us to filter by geographic areas.

From this data we can observe that over the last 8 months there has been a 10% increase in individuals searching for nearby Animal Shelters!

Curiously though, the increase is not exponential like the rise in COVID-19 cases in the U.S. that we saw in the last graph. Maybe people aren't interested in adopting from a shelter and would rather buy from a breeder.
