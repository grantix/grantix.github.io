Are you a cat person or a dog person?

Instead of jumping right to animal shelters, maybe the first step in finding a Pandemic Pet is choosing a species or a breed. The graph below can be toggled with buttons between the search trends for 'Cats' or 'Dogs'.

Try it out for yourself!

The most interesting observation from this data is that searches for both cats and dogs spiked around March 22. This was one week after most U.S. companies decided to go remote!

As most U.S. companies are still working-from-home, the search trend remains significatly elevated. And sorry to all the canine lovers out there...cats are searched for about 10% more than dogs are this year.

Regardless of the search terms and popularity contests, bringing a pet into your family is a big decision... and it costs money!
